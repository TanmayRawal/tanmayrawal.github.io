INSIGHT-BRAIN: Interpretable Neural Systems using Grad-CAM, SHAP, and LIME in Human-Centered Brain Tumor Imaging
Published

Human-centered XAI framework pairing three CNN architectures with three interpretability methods — InceptionV3+Grad-CAM for spatial heatmaps, Xception+LIME for feature attribution, EfficientNetB0+SHAP for global contributions. Benchmarked against fidelity, stability, and cross-method consistency. Because a clinician's trust demands explanations that don't contradict each other.
